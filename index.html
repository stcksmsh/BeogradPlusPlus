BeogradPlusPlus - Free public transport

Is the app meant for fare evasion

No. The app made to point out an issue in the payment system, and that is its sole and only purpose.

Does it still work?

No. A new system for checking the ticket numbers was introduced in early October of 2023, rendering the app pretty much useless.

Will there be updates?

It depends. I am planning to do some research, and try to figure out a way to clone valid ticket numbers, but it is not something I will be doing soon.

Will there be an IOS version

No. I can make an IOS application, but for anyone to be able to install it I would need to pay Apple 99$ a year for an Apple Developer Membership. The app would also be difficult to install on your IPhone, as it would not be available on the app store.

About the project



BeogradPlusPlus is an android app made to point out a serious flaw which existed in the city of Belgrades public transport payment system. The system worked by the riders sending messages to the number '9011' which contained the ticket type they wished to purchase, and then they would receive a confirmation message, containing a ticket number if the payment was successful. This pattern of messages was easy to fake as there was really no feasable way to check whether someone had actually purchased a ticket, because there was (at the time) no central database through the use of which ticket number could be cross checked. Someone riding the various busses and trams throughout the city could simply use an app like this, or text a friend whom they saved in their contacts as '9011'. The problem has been fixed as of October 2023, but this app serves as a testament to poor software security which happens every day.
